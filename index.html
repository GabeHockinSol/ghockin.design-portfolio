Planning

When reading the brief, I started to break down each section and take notes so I would gain a clear idea of what the outcome should look like. I also looked at the grading system for this task and took it into consideration to help me understand and evaluate how my portfolio should look.

Throughout the brief it states that planning is a necessary element for this task. I decided to make a flow chart diagram using “Lucid Chart” to help me manage each component to the project. Although this is not an industry favoured way of planning a project compared to other methodologies such as Agile development, I personally felt that this would help me plan and keep track of my work.
